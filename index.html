About Us!

New Apparel Inc. is an online-only retailer and former brick-and-mortar stores operator based in La Pampa, Argentina. Founded by Argentinian businessman Agustin in 2023, it was a vertically integrated company that ranked as one of the largest apparel manufacturers and marketers in South America.
